# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface, derived directly from our design tokens.

## Brand Identity

*   **Primary Color:** `#2665fd` - The brand's most distinctive chromatic color, suitable for buttons, CTAs, and key interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#6074b9` - A supporting color for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#bd3800` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#757681` - A neutral base color for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements.
*   **Color Mode:** Light

## Typography

*   **Headlines:** `Space Grotesk` - Used for all major headings and titles to convey a modern and impactful presence.
*   **Body Text:** `Space Grotesk` - Applied to paragraphs and general text, ensuring readability and consistency with the headline style.
*   **Labels:** `Space Grotesk` - Utilized for form labels, small text, and UI annotations for clear identification.

## Shape and Form

*   **Roundedness:** Moderate roundedness for UI elements, striking a balance between modern aesthetics and approachability.

## Layout and Spacing

*   **Spacing:** Normal level of whitespace, providing a balanced and comfortable visual density for most applications.
